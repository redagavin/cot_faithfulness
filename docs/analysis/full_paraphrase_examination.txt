The paraphrasing quality is assessed across three dimensions:
1. CONSERVATIVENESS: How much the sentence changed
2. REPLACEMENT QUALITY: Whether the sentence swap worked correctly
3. OVERALL RATING: EXCELLENT (>90% retention, <15% length change)
                   GOOD (>75% retention, <30% length change)
                   MODERATE (>60% retention, <50% length change)

Most paraphrases should be EXCELLENT or GOOD to ensure
we're measuring baseline sensitivity, not semantic changes.
